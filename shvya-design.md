# Shvya AI - Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

### Brand Identity
**Shvya AI** represents cutting-edge technology with a focus on rapid deployment and intelligent automation. The design reflects innovation, reliability, and speed while maintaining professional credibility.

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Navy (#1a1d29) - Trust, technology, professionalism
- **Secondary**: Electric Blue (#0066ff) - Innovation, AI, digital transformation  
- **Accent**: Bright Orange (#ff6b35) - Speed, urgency (4hr deployment), energy
- **Neutral**: Light Gray (#f8f9fa) - Clean, minimal background
- **Text**: Charcoal (#2d3748) - High contrast, readable
- **Success**: Green (#10b981) - Positive actions, confirmations

### Typography
- **Display Font**: "Inter" - Modern, geometric, tech-forward for headings
- **Body Font**: "Inter" - Consistent, highly readable for content
- **Code Font**: "JetBrains Mono" - For technical elements and demos
- **Brand Font**: Bold weight for "Shvya" to emphasize strength

### Visual Language
- **AI-First Design**: Elements that suggest artificial intelligence and automation
- **Speed & Efficiency**: Dynamic animations, quick transitions, performance-focused
- **Professional Tech**: Corporate-friendly with modern startup energy
- **Data-Driven**: Charts, metrics, and analytics visualizations
- **Minimalist Tech**: Clean lines, generous whitespace, geometric precision

## Visual Effects

### Used Libraries
- **Anime.js**: Smooth micro-interactions and element animations
- **ECharts.js**: Interactive data visualizations and AI metrics
- **p5.js**: Dynamic background with AI-inspired particle systems
- **Splide.js**: Product showcase carousels and image galleries
- **Matter.js**: Physics-based animations for floating elements
- **Pixi.js**: Advanced visual effects and shader backgrounds

### Effects & Animations
- **Hero Background**: AI-inspired neural network visualization with flowing connections
- **Text Effects**: Typewriter animation for main headline, color cycling for AI emphasis
- **Product Cards**: 3D tilt effect on hover, holographic preview overlays
- **Data Visualization**: Real-time animated charts showing AI performance metrics
- **Loading States**: AI-themed loading animations with neural network patterns

### Styling Approach
- **Glassmorphism**: Semi-transparent cards with backdrop blur effects
- **Gradient Accents**: Subtle gradients on interactive elements
- **Neon Highlights**: Electric blue glow effects on AI-powered features
- **Shadow Design**: Layered shadows for depth and modern appeal
- **Responsive Grid**: Flexible layouts that adapt to all screen sizes

### Header Effect
- **Dynamic Navigation**: Smooth color transitions on scroll
- **Sticky Header**: Persistent access to key actions with blur background
- **AI Status Indicator**: Live indicator showing system status
- **Progress Timeline**: Shows deployment progress for 4-hour delivery

### Interactive Elements
- **Live Previews**: Interactive product demos with realistic AI interfaces
- **Smart Filters**: AI-powered product recommendation system
- **Comparison Matrix**: Dynamic product comparison with AI insights
- **Contact Forms**: Real-time validation with AI-powered suggestions
