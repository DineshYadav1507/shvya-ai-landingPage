## Technical Implementation

### Core Libraries
- **Anime.js**: Micro-interactions and AI-themed animations
- **ECharts.js**: Data visualizations and AI performance metrics
- **Splide.js**: Product carousels and AI showcase galleries
- **p5.js**: Dynamic AI neural network background effects
- **Matter.js**: Physics-based animations for floating AI elements
- **Pixi.js**: Advanced visual effects and AI shader backgrounds

### Performance Optimization
- **Lazy Loading**: Progressive AI content loading
- **Code Splitting**: Separate JS files for different AI functionality
- **Caching Strategy**: Smart caching for AI models and data
- **CDN Integration**: Fast loading of AI libraries and resources

### Responsive Design
- **Mobile First**: Optimized for mobile AI interactions
- **Tablet Adaptation**: Enhanced layouts for tablet viewing
- **Desktop Experience**: Full-featured desktop AI interface
- **High DPI**: Retina and high-resolution AI display support
